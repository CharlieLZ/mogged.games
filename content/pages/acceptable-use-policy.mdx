This Acceptable Use Policy explains the rules that apply when you use mogged on `mogged.games`, including public pages, accounts, face rating game battles, browser tools, credits, subscriptions, and related support flows.

## Key points upfront

- Use the service lawfully, honestly, and within the product boundaries described on this site.
- Do not use mogged to generate, upload, share, or request harmful, illegal, deceptive, or infringing content.
- Read this page together with our [Terms of Service](/terms-of-service), [Content Moderation Policy](/content-moderation-policy), and [Refund Policy](/refund-policy) when you need the full picture.

## What this policy covers

This policy applies to:

- accounts, billing, and support interactions
- face rating game battles and mog comparison features
- uploaded selfies, comparison photos, reference images, and resulting battle ratings
- browser-based tools offered on `mogged.games`
- automation, scripted usage, and any attempt to use the service at scale

## General use principles

You must:

- comply with applicable law
- respect privacy, intellectual property, and consent requirements
- provide accurate information when creating an account or purchasing credits
- keep your account credentials secure
- use the service in ways that do not create unreasonable abuse, fraud, security, or payment risk

## Prohibited content and requests

You may not use mogged to request, upload, create, or distribute content involving:

- sexual or exploitative content, including any sexualized minor content
- graphic violence, torture, self-harm promotion, or threats
- hate speech, extremist propaganda, or targeted harassment
- fraud, impersonation, scams, deepfakes meant to deceive, or harmful misinformation
- illegal drugs, trafficking, weapons for unlawful use, or instructions for criminal conduct
- copyright, trademark, or publicity-right violations where you do not have the required rights or permissions

## Prohibited behavior

You may not:

- try to bypass moderation or safety filters
- scrape, overload, or disrupt the platform
- create multiple accounts to evade restrictions
- abuse credits, refunds, chargebacks, coupons, or payment flows
- reverse engineer, probe, or interfere with the service or its providers
- use automated access in ways that exceed documented or intended product boundaries

## User responsibility

You remain responsible for the uploads, comparison photos, ratings, and downstream uses connected to your account. If your work involves real people, brands, copyrighted material, or sensitive subject matter, you are responsible for having the necessary rights and permissions before using the service.

## Enforcement

Depending on severity and frequency, mogged may:

- block the request
- warn the account
- limit access to parts of the service
- suspend or terminate the account
- deny refunds where policy abuse is involved
- report unlawful conduct when required by law
