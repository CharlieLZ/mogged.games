## mogged Leaderboard

The mogged leaderboard is the public ranking surface for 1v1 face rating battles on `mogged.games`.

### What the leaderboard shows

- **ELO movement** after eligible mog battles
- **Rank tiers** from Molecule to Slayer
- **Battle-ready profile context** that helps users understand recent progress
- **Leaderboard status** for competitive face rating sessions

### Product boundary

Leaderboard data belongs to the mogged game layer. Browser tools, profile prep, billing, and support remain separate parts of the same `mogged.games` product.
