Effective date: April 10, 2026

This Refund Policy explains how mogged handles refunds for orders placed on `mogged.games`.

## Key points upfront

- Unused one-time credits requested within **3 calendar days** may be reviewed for refund.
- Used credits, completed battles, and already-consumed subscription benefits are generally not refundable.
- Subscription cancellation stops future renewals. It does not automatically reverse the current billing period.
- If you are comparing the refund boundary against the product promise, read this page together with [Pricing](/pricing), [Terms of Service](/terms-of-service), [Mission](/mission), and [Acceptable Use Policy](/acceptable-use-policy).

## Scope

This policy applies to purchases made from our site, such as:

- one-time credit packages
- recurring subscriptions
- paid access to face rating game capacity or related site services

This policy does **not** govern third-party vendor billing, external API charges, or any purchase made outside `mogged.games`.

## General rule

Because battles and comparisons can trigger provider costs quickly, completed or partially used purchases are generally **not refundable** unless required by law or caused by a clear billing or service failure on our side.

## Eligible refund scenarios

You may be eligible for a refund if:

1. You were charged more than once for the same order.
2. A payment succeeded but the related credits or subscription access were never delivered.
3. A technical failure prevented a purchased battle or comparison from completing and the credits were not restored.
4. You request a refund for an unused one-time credit purchase within **3 calendar days** of purchase.
5. Law in your jurisdiction requires a refund.

## Non-refundable cases

Refunds are generally not available when:

- purchased credits have already been used in whole or in part
- subscription benefits have already been used for the billed period
- battle results were delivered and the request is based only on preference, disagreement with ratings, or a change of mind
- the issue was caused by misuse, policy violations, or unsupported inputs
- the request is made after the refund window stated above, unless law requires otherwise

## Subscription cancellation

You can cancel a subscription to stop future renewals. Cancellation does not automatically create a refund for the current billing cycle, except where required by law or explicitly approved under this policy.

## How to request a refund

Email <SupportEmailLink /> with:

1. your account email
2. order number or transaction ID
3. purchase date
4. the reason for the request
5. screenshots or error details if the issue is technical

## Review and processing

We aim to respond within **3 business days**. If approved, refunds are typically sent back to the original payment method. Final posting time depends on your payment provider.

## Chargebacks

If you believe a charge is incorrect, contact us before filing a chargeback when possible. Early contact usually resolves billing issues faster and reduces accidental account restrictions.

## Policy changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The revised version becomes effective when posted on this page.
