## What mogged is for

### Our mission

Our mission is simple: let anyone jump into a 1v1 face-off, get rated by AI, and climb the leaderboard. No filters, no fluff — just you, your opponent, and the mog. mogged was built because everyone deserves a shot at being the mogger, not the mogged.

### How it works

1. **Camera Check** — Complete a quick webcam check to get started.
2. **Face Scan** — Our AI scans your face and measures key biometric traits.
3. **Mog Battle** — Get matched against a random opponent in a 1v1 face rating battle.
4. **Climb the Ranks** — Win matches, earn ELO points, and climb from Molecule to Slayer.

### Why mogged

A face rating platform should feel like stepping into an arena, not filling out a survey. You see your opponent, the AI scans both faces, and the mogger is crowned in seconds. No complicated setup, no hidden rules — just a clean, fast mog battle.

We built the ranking system to reward consistency. Every match moves your ELO. Beat higher-ranked players to climb faster. Stay active to hold your position. The leaderboard updates in real time so you always know where you stand.

### Who this is for

- streamers and content creators looking for engaging mog battle content
- looksmaxxing enthusiasts tracking their progress
- casual players who just want quick, fun 1v1 face-offs
