This Privacy Policy explains how mogged collects, uses, stores, and shares personal information when you visit `mogged.games`, read our public pages, create an account, buy credits or subscriptions, contact support, or use our face rating game and browser-based game tools.

## Key points upfront

- This policy covers `mogged.games` and the services sold on this site, not third-party purchases made elsewhere.
- Browser tools may keep files on your device, while face rating battle features may send prompts, files, or task settings to our service providers.
- Read this page together with our [Pricing](/pricing), [Mission](/mission), [Terms of Service](/terms-of-service), and [AI Wrapper Disclaimer](/ai-wrapper-disclaimer) pages when you want the full service boundary.

## What parts of the service this policy covers

This policy applies to:

- public content pages such as workflow guides, FAQ, and pricing notes
- account, billing, and support flows on `mogged.games`
- face rating battle features where uploaded photos, comparison settings, or battle results are processed by us or our service providers
- browser tools that may process files locally in your device browser when the feature is designed that way

## What we collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

1. **Account data**
   - Name, email address, login identifiers, and profile details you provide.

2. **Workspace inputs and uploads**
   - Prompts, uploaded files, reference assets, task settings, task history, and resulting battle ratings associated with your use of the face rating game.

3. **Usage and device data**
   - IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers, referral data, timestamps, page views, and product interaction events.

4. **Payment and billing data**
   - Order details, transaction status, subscription state, billing country, and limited payment metadata.
   - Payment card details are generally processed by our payment partners, not stored by us in full.

5. **Support and communication data**
   - Emails, chat content, issue descriptions, screenshots, and other information you send when you ask for help.

6. **Cookies and analytics data**
   - Cookies, local storage, and measurement technologies used for authentication, preferences, fraud prevention, and product analytics.

## How we use information

We use personal information to:

- operate the public site, localize content, and measure which mogged topics people actually need
- operate accounts, billing, credits, subscriptions, and task history
- process uploads, photo comparisons, reference-based rating tasks, and other game features
- provide browser tools and product features
- prevent abuse, fraud, chargebacks, spam, and security incidents
- improve product quality, reliability, and support operations
- send transactional emails and service notices
- comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms

## When we share information

We may share information with:

- infrastructure, hosting, storage, analytics, email, and customer support vendors
- payment processors and tax or anti-fraud partners
- AI or rating processing providers when needed to complete the battle or comparison you asked us to run
- professional advisers or authorities when required by law, to defend rights, or to investigate misuse
- a successor entity if the business or assets are reorganized, sold, or transferred

We do **not** sell your personal information for cash consideration.

## Local browser tools

Some tools on `mogged.games` are designed to run locally in the browser. In those cases, files may stay on your device unless you separately choose to upload or share them through another feature. Local processing does not mean zero risk, so you should still review your own environment, browser extensions, and device security.

## Data retention

We keep information for as long as reasonably necessary to operate the service, maintain security and accounting records, resolve disputes, and comply with law. Retention periods may vary based on account status, order history, fraud review, legal requests, and backup cycles.

## Security

We use administrative, technical, and organizational measures designed to protect personal information. No internet service can be guaranteed 100% secure, so you should also protect your account credentials and contact us promptly if you suspect unauthorized access.

## International transfers

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own, depending on where our vendors and infrastructure operate.

## Your choices and rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to request access, correction, deletion, export, or restriction of certain personal information. You may also be able to object to certain processing. To make a request, email <SupportEmailLink /> from the account address you used on the service.

## Children

mogged is not directed to children under 13, and you should not use the service if you are not old enough to form a binding agreement in your jurisdiction.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The updated version becomes effective when posted on this page, with the effective date revised above.
